Telestration Roadmap

A 21-point architectural plan for elite sports analysis and immersive storytelling.

Phase 1: Infrastructure, UX & Persistence
01

HLS Infrastructure & J-K-L Scrubbing Complete

Frame-accurate transport controls (J: Rewind, K: Pause, L: Fast-Forward) for 40Mbps streams.

02

Temporal Pinning

Logic to assign 'In' and 'Out' points to graphics so they appear/disappear based on the HLS clock.

03

Tool Property Bar Complete

Real-time modification of color, thickness, and fill for selected tactical annotations.

04

Undo/Redo Stack

Standardized transactional state management (Ctrl+Z/Ctrl+Y) for all annotation actions.

05

Progressive Reveal & Drawing Animation UX Logic

Configure reveal styles (Draw-on, Fade-on, or Instant) as a per-annotation or per-type option.

06

Annotation Serialization & Playback Complete

Persistence engine to record tactical sequences and auto-load them based on video filename.

07

JSON Data Export Complete

One-click clipboard export of the entire tactical sequence for sharing and external analysis.

08

Sequence Management & Selection

UI for checking in external tactical files and hot-swapping between multiple reviews.

09

View Snapshot & Export

Context-aware image capture (Full TB, Left, or Right views) with burned-in annotations.

10

Movement Echo Trails

Display a fading 'ghost' path representing a player's historical trajectory over time.

11

Tactical Voice Annotation

Synchronized audio narration recording tied to the video timeline and tactical highlights.

Phase 2: Coordinate Engine, AI & Perspective
12

Coordinate-Aware Canvas Homography

Foundation for mapping 2D screen coordinates to 3D pitch space using normalized vectors.

13

Field Corner Calibration Homography

Dual-view calibration using hollow donuts to compute the View-to-View matrix (H_LR).

14

Perspective Tactical Arrow Homography

Ground-tethered vectors that narrow toward the vanishing point for professional broadcast aesthetic.

15

Tracking Player Halo Homography

'Open Base' highlights rendered behind player legs with perspective-correct scaling.

16

AI Snap-to-Player AI / Tracking

Automatic centering of highlights using real-time object detection and bounding box alignment.

17

3D Occupancy Zones Homography

Perspective polygons with 'Spotlight Mode' to isolate tactical blocks and scoring areas.

18

Biometric Data Cards Data Fusion

Real-time performance metrics (speed, HR) tethered to tracked player halos in 3D space.

19

Depth Anchoring Homography

Z-plane alignment tool to synchronize graphic depth with player baselines.

Phase 3: Quest 3 Immersive Support
20

Stereoscopic WebXR Playback Quest 3

3D viewing of Top-Bottom HLS streams with depth-accurate spatial annotations and parallax.

21

Spatial Hand/Controller Input Quest 3

Immersive tactical editing using Quest 3 spatial input and passthrough for real-world contextual drawing.
